## Primary Goal

Synthesize the outputs of Prompts 01-04 into one detailed, granular, step-by-step execution plan across all dimensions while keeping the true priority order intact:

1. win near-term interviews
2. secure a strong job outcome
3. build brand and market credibility in parallel
4. validate a small consulting / automation / agency path without derailing the first three

Preserve these goals and tensions:

- short term: pass interviews, apply more, gather experience
- mid term: get multiple offers and choose a high-paying remote role
- long term: gain freedom, autonomy, and flexibility to build an imperfect but viable agency
- execution philosophy: act early, learn fast, improve later
- question to resolve explicitly: which workstreams should remain parallel and which must stay sequential
