Head of Engineering

Do you thrive on building teams, systems, and products that make a measurable difference?

You'll have the opportunity to:
• Define and deliver the technical roadmap for an AI-driven building platform that learns, predicts, and optimises energy performance in real time.

• Shape architectural and product decisions in close collaboration with Head of Product and engineering leads.

This is a unique opportunity to build a platform and a team from the ground up in a domain where technology directly contributes to a more sustainable future. If you're a hands-on engineering leader who values clarity, craft, and purpose, while aligning with business goals, we'd love to hear from you.

What you'll do:
• Lead the design, architecture, and delivery of cMatter's agentic AI platform from data ingestion and backend services to AI orchestration and frontend integration.
• Collaborate closely with Product, Data Ingestion and Data Science to translate product vision and customer problems into scalable technical solutions.
• Build and mentor a multidisciplinary engineering team across software, data, and AI disciplines, fostering a culture of technical excellence, ownership, and learning.
• Define engineering standards, review critical designs and code, and ensure the reliability, scalability, and cost-efficiency of all systems.
• Drive adoption of best practices in CI/CD, observability, automated testing, and secure-by-design development.
• Partner with leadership on roadmap planning, resource allocation, and technical hiring strategy.
• Play a key role in transforming early-stage pilots into a production-grade SaaS platform deployed across multiple building portfolios.
