# Sr. Manager Data & AI Tech AI/ML Platform Tech Lead
**Company:** dsm-firmenich
**Location:** Barcelona, Spain or Hyderabad, India

## Responsibilities
- Lead the design and implementation of cloud-based AI/ML platforms (Azure or AWS) to support model training, deployment, and inference at scale
- Architect and maintain end-to-end ML pipelines for data ingestion, preprocessing, training, validation, and production deployment
- Implement and optimize agentic AI frameworks and workflows for advanced autonomous decision-making and orchestration
- Drive DevOps and MLOps best practices, including CI/CD for ML models, monitoring, observability, and rollback strategies
- Manage Kubernetes-based deployments, ensuring high availability, scalability, and security of AI/ML services
- Define and enforce cost-optimization strategies for compute, storage, and model-serving infrastructure
- Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and stakeholders to translate business requirements into scalable AI platform solutions
- Provide technical leadership, mentorship, and architectural guidance to engineering teams

- 7+ years of experience in software engineering, DevOps, or platform engineering with a focus on cloud-native environments (AWS or Azure)
- Proven track record in designing and operating AI/ML infrastructure supporting model training and large-scale inference
- Strong expertise with Kubernetes and containerized workloads in production
- Experience building and maintaining CI/CD pipelines for ML/AI systems
- In-depth knowledge of agentic AI concepts and frameworks, with practical implementation experience
- Strong background in infrastructure cost management and optimization within cloud environments
- Excellent problem-solving, leadership, and communication skills
