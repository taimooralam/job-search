## 11. Product Details

### What Sastrify Does

Sastrify is an **AI-powered SaaS management and procurement platform** that combines software with human procurement expertise.

### Core Capabilities

**1. Software Discovery & Visibility**
- Connects to IDPs, browsers, ERPs for automatic tool discovery
- Shadow IT detection
- Usage analytics (identify unused seats, overlapping tools)
- 90% vendor/spend visibility within 7 days

**2. Procurement & Negotiation**
- Intake-to-procure workflow automation
- Expert-supported purchasing and renewals
- Pricing benchmarks against $6B+ in live deal data
- 15,000+ vendors auto-mapped
- 30% average savings across software landscapes

**3. Contract & Renewal Management**
- AI Contract Extractor (auto-parses pricing tiers, auto-renewal clauses, SLAs)
- Automated renewal alerts and approvals
- Vendor tracking and lifecycle management

**4. Compliance & Governance**
- Auto-documentation of vendor compliance status
- DORA, NIS2, GDPR, AI Act compliance support
- One-click audit report generation
- Certificate attachment and management

**5. Sastrify Marketplace**
- Discounted rates and preferred terms from SaaS vendors
- Flexible payment and financing options (partnership with Capchase)

**6. Integrations**
- Google Workspace, Xero, NetSuite, Jira
- IDP, browser, ERP connectors

### Customer Results
- **Sennder:** $200K saved
- **Bitvavo:** EUR 400K saved
- **6 hours** weekly time savings per team
- **15x ROI** reported by some customers

## Key Takeaways for Interview Prep

### Strengths to Reference
- Strong product-market fit in European SaaS procurement
- Timely compliance angle (DORA, NIS2, AI Act) driving enterprise demand
- Two successful acquisitions show strategic execution
- $6B+ pricing benchmark dataset is a real moat
- AI-powered features differentiating from manual competitors

### Potential Concerns / Questions to Ask
1. **Team size reduction:** Company went from ~150 to ~60-70 employees. What's the current trajectory?
2. **Small engineering team:** Only ~7-8 engineers. Is the plan to grow? What's the hiring roadmap?
3. **No funding since late 2023:** Is the company profitable or planning another raise?
4. **CTO is business-background:** Max has a business degree + bootcamp. How does engineering leadership work day-to-day?
5. **Low-code development:** The "Low Code Developer" role suggests parts of the platform may be built on low-code tools. What's the architecture?
6. **Onboarding concerns:** Multiple Glassdoor reviews flag poor onboarding. How has this improved?

### Your Angle
- **Engineering leadership at scale:** Experience leading teams and building scalable systems directly addresses their need for an Engineering Lead
- **AI/ML expertise:** Their pivot toward AI-powered features (benchmarking, contract extraction, compliance) aligns with your AI background
- **Remote-first experience:** They operate CET-1 to CET+1, remote-first
- **SaaS platform experience:** B2B SaaS is exactly this company's domain
