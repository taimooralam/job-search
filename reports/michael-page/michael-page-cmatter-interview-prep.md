### Very Early Stage Signals

| Signal | Risk | Follow-up Question |
|--------|------|-------------------|
| "Pre-product" | May be 12-24 months from revenue | "What's the timeline to production customers?" |
| "First engineer hire" | May be doing everything yourself | "What's the hiring roadmap?" |

| Signal | Risk | Response |
|--------|------|----------|
| Range below AED 45k | Undervaluing role | "That's below market. Is there flexibility?" |
| "We don't do equity" | Limited upside | Consider higher base |
| Vague on allowances | May not be standard | "Can you break down the total package?" |

| Signal | Risk | Response |
