## 1. Freelance Platform Research

### Fiverr (incl. Fiverr Pro / Business)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Seller Commission** | 20% flat on all earnings (incl. tips). 5% on portion of single order exceeding $500. No tiers, no volume discounts |
| **Buyer Fee** | 5.5% service fee + $3 "small-order" fee if order < $100 |
| **Trustpilot** | 3.5/5 (~15,000 reviews). 60% 5-star, 26% 1-star |
| **Best For** | Quick deliverables, creative/design, smaller AI tasks, productized services |
| **Fiverr Pro** | Vetted talent tier; premium plans and Team Account available for growing businesses |

**Pros for AI/Technical Consultants:**
- Massive client volume; strong for productized AI offerings (chatbots, agent dev)
- AI agent demand up 18,347% in freelancer searches (Spring 2025 BTI)
- ~30% of top AI consultant orders are now for AI agent development
- Personal AI program: freelancers can create AI models trained on their work

**Cons:**
- 20% commission is the highest among major platforms
- Race-to-the-bottom pricing culture
- AI disruption hitting revenue: Fiverr forecasting decline to $380-420M in 2026 (down from $430M in 2025)
- 25% workforce layoffs announced (Sept 2025)
- Brand perception as "cheap" work

**Recent News:** Fiverr pivoting to "AI-first strategy" with 25% adjusted EBITDA margin target for 2026. Fiverr Ads expanded into Pro catalog.

## 3. EU Inc Concept

### What Is It?
A proposed "28th regime" -- a new pan-European company legal form that operates alongside (not replacing) existing national company structures in all 27 EU member states.

### Current Status (March 2026)
- **Proposed:** March 18, 2026 by the European Commission
- **NOT yet law:** Requires approval by European Parliament and Council of the EU
- **Timeline:** Commission calling for agreement by end of 2026. First registrations anticipated Q1 2027
- **Origin:** Announced by EU Commission President von der Leyen at WEF Davos, January 2025

### Key Features
| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Registration** | Fully digital, within 48 hours, from anywhere in the world |
| **Cost** | < EUR 100 |
| **Minimum Capital** | EUR 1 (lowest in Europe) |
| **Notary** | Not required |
| **Language** | All documentation in English |
| **Scope** | Single entity operating across all 27 EU member states |
| **Citizenship** | No EU citizenship or residency required |
| **Investment Docs** | Standardized EU-FAST investment documents |
| **Stock Options** | Harmonized EU-ESOP framework with deferred taxation |
| **Insolvency** | Fully digital insolvency procedures |
| **Data** | "Once-only" principle: submit info once, valid across all jurisdictions |

### Comparison with Existing Options

| Feature | EU Inc | Societas Europaea (SE) | National Ltd |
|---------|--------|----------------------|--------------|
| Target | Startups & SMEs | Large public companies | Any |
| Min Capital | EUR 1 | EUR 120,000 | Varies (EUR 1 - 25,000+) |
| Registration | Digital, 48 hours | Complex, months | Days to weeks |
| Cross-border | Seamless across 27 states | Complex | Per-country |
| Notary | Not required | Usually required | Usually required |
| Cost | < EUR 100 | Thousands | Hundreds to thousands |

### Projected Impact
- 300,000 companies created in first 10 years
- 10% of new companies using EU Inc by year 10
- 1.6 million jobs created

### Verdict
Not available yet. If adopted as proposed, this would be a game-changer for freelancers/consultants wanting pan-EU presence with minimal bureaucracy. The EUR 1 minimum capital and 48-hour digital registration are dramatically more accessible than any existing option.

## 4. UAE Freelancer Visa / Malta Setup

### UAE Freelance Permits

#### Visa Types & Costs

| Option | Cost (AED) | Cost (EUR approx.) | Duration |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|----------|
| GoFreelance (TECOM) | 7,500 - 12,000 | ~1,800 - 2,900 | 1-2 years |
| Dubai Talent Pass | 9,500 | ~2,300 | 2 years |
| Remote Work Visa | 7,000 - 12,000 | ~1,700 - 2,900 | 1 year |
| Full Freelance Visa (2yr) | 12,500 - 30,000 | ~3,000 - 7,300 | 2 years |

*Costs typically include: freelance permit, establishment card, medical tests, residency visa processing*

#### Tax Structure
- **Personal income tax:** 0%
- **Corporate tax:** 9% on profits exceeding AED 375,000 (~EUR 90,000)
- **No:** property tax, dividend tax, capital gains tax for individuals
- **VAT:** 5% on most goods/services

#### Free Zone Options
- **Dubai:** TECOM clusters (GoFreelance), Dubai Internet City, Dubai Media City
- **Abu Dhabi:** ADGM, Twofour54, ADEDD
- **Budget options:** RAKEZ (Ras Al Khaimah), SHAMS (Sharjah Media City)

#### 2025-2026 Updates
- Tighter compliance checks and documentation rules
- Regular compliance reviews for visa holders
- New documentation standards for renewals

#### Requirements
1. Freelance permit/license from a government authority
2. UAE residence visa based on that permit
3. Medical tests
4. Emirates ID

**Verdict:** Attractive for 0% personal income tax. The 9% corporate tax threshold of AED 375K is reasonable. Best for consultants billing EUR 100K+/year who want tax efficiency. Dubai's TECOM/GoFreelance is the most established option for tech freelancers.

### Malta Company Formation

#### Setup Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Timeline** | As little as 24 hours for EU citizens |
| **Formation Cost** | From EUR 980 (including bank account, tax/VAT registration) |
| **Minimum Share Capital** | EUR 1,165 |
| **Monthly Accounting** | From EUR 160/month |
| **Effective Tax Rate** | 5% (via Malta tax refund system) |
| **Corporate Tax (nominal)** | 35% (but 6/7ths refundable to non-resident shareholders = 5% effective) |

#### Requirements
- Company name selection
- Memorandum and Articles of Association
- Registered office in Malta
- Company secretary appointment
- Shareholder and director identification
- Bank references
- Submit to Malta Business Registry (MBR)

#### Advantages
- Full EU member state (EU passporting rights)
- English-speaking business environment
- 5% effective corporate tax (lowest in EU via refund mechanism)
- Strong financial regulation
- Political stability
- No minimum residency requirement to own a company
- Access to EU single market and double tax treaty network

#### Costs Summary (Annual)
- Formation: ~EUR 1,000 (one-time)
- Registered office: ~EUR 1,000-2,000/year
- Accounting/bookkeeping: ~EUR 2,000-4,000/year
- Annual return filing: ~EUR 300-500
- **Total annual running cost: ~EUR 3,500-7,000**

**Verdict:** Malta is the most tax-efficient EU company formation option at 5% effective rate. Fast setup for EU citizens. Good for routing consulting income through a legitimate EU entity. Pairs well with UAE residency (Malta company + UAE personal residency = near-zero tax structure).
