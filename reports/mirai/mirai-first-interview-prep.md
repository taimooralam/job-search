- The tax year you move, you'll pay German tax on income earned until departure

## 3. Visa & Relocation Guide

### 3.1 Saudi Work Visa Process (German Nationals)

**Overview:** Employer-sponsored system. Mirai handles everything — you just provide documents.

| Step | What Happens | Timeline |
|------|-------------|----------|
| 1. **Job Offer Accepted** | Sign contract | Day 0 |
| 2. **Block Visa Quota** | Mirai applies to MHRSD for visa quota (with skill-tier classification) | 2-4 weeks |
| 3. **MOFA Authorization** | Ministry of Foreign Affairs issues visa authorization | 2-3 weeks |
| 4. **Visa Stamping** | You visit Saudi Embassy in Berlin/Munich with documents | 1-2 weeks |
| 5. **Travel to Riyadh** | Enter Saudi Arabia on work visa | Day ~50-60 |
| 6. **Iqama Issuance** | Residency permit (ID card) issued within 90 days | 1-2 weeks after arrival |
| **Total** | **Offer to working in Riyadh** | **~6-10 weeks** |

**Your Skill Tier:** High-Skilled (degree + 5+ years experience + SAR 15,000+ salary) — fastest processing.

**Required Documents:**
- Valid German passport (6+ months validity)
- Degree certificates (attested/apostilled)
- Employment contract from Mirai
- Medical exam (done in KSA after arrival)
- Passport photos
- Police clearance certificate (Führungszeugnis)

**Iqama (Residency Permit):**
- Your legal ID in Saudi Arabia
- Needed for: banking, renting, mobile phone contract, travel within/out of KSA
- Employer-managed renewal (annual)
- Cost: 650 SAR/year + ~500 SAR medical exams (employer typically pays)
