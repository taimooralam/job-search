## 1. Company Overview

### What is FYUL?

**FYUL** (pronounced "fuel") is the newly formed global on-demand commerce powerhouse created through the merger of three companies:

| Brand | Specialization | Founded |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| **Printify** | Print-on-demand marketplace platform | 2015, Riga, Latvia |
| **Printful** | Print-on-demand production & fulfillment | 2013, California (HQ in Riga) |
| **Snow Commerce** | Premium merchandise & enterprise solutions | 2010, Cincinnati, USA |

### Company Vision

> "For FYUL to power all eCommerce merchandise, made on-demand"

FYUL brings together:
- **Technology** - Platform connecting sellers to print providers

- **1+ million products** manufactured and shipped per month

- Moving to a **97,000 sq ft facility in Riga** (Q1 2026)
